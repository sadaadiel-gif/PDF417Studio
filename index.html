How it works

From fields to barcode
in four steps.

1

Select your state

Choose any US state — IIN and Subfile Length auto-fill from the built-in AAMVA database. Or enter a custom IIN manually.

2

Fill the form

Enter name, licence number, dates, address and physical attributes. Sex, State, and First Tag are all dropdowns.

3

Generate AAMVA

Click Generate — the compliant AAMVA payload appears instantly. Switch to the PDF417 tab and click AAMVA Preset for safe defaults.

4

Export

Save as PNG with embedded DPI metadata, or export a single-page PDF matching the on-screen preview exactly.
